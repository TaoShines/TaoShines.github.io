About me

I graduated from the Physics Department of Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2021. After that, I studied under Professor Wang Yan and continued to study for a doctorate in astrophysics at Huazhong University of Science and Technology. The current main research direction is the detection of gravitational waves by the pulsar timing array. The current research focuses on the improvement of the detection algorithm of the pulsar timing array for the ringdown and gravitational wave memory. In the future, the real data of FAST will be used to limit the parameters with astronomical significance.
